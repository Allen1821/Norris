About AS9100 Certification

AS9100 is the standardized quality management system for the aerospace industry. It builds upon ISO 9001 requirements with additional criteria specific to aviation, space, and defense organizations. At Norris Precision Manufacturing, we've maintained our AS9100 certification since 2005, demonstrating our commitment to the highest quality standards required by aerospace and defense prime contractors.

Why AS9100 Matters

AS9100 certification ensures that our quality management system meets the rigorous requirements of the aerospace industry, which includes:

  • Enhanced product safety requirements
  • Rigorous risk management processes
  • Configuration management controls
  • Prevention of counterfeit parts
  • Product conformity and on-time delivery focus
  • Detailed control of external providers

Our Certification Journey

Norris Precision Manufacturing has maintained AS9100 certification for over 15 years. Our quality management system undergoes regular surveillance audits and recertification every three years by accredited third-party registrars. We consistently demonstrate compliance with aerospace industry requirements while continuously improving our processes to exceed customer expectations.

Benefits to Our Customers

Our AS9100 certification provides our customers with assurance that:

  • All products are manufactured under strict quality controls
  • Processes are standardized and consistently applied
  • Risk management is integrated into all operations
  • Product safety and conformity are prioritized
  • Traceability and documentation meet aerospace requirements
  • Continuous improvement drives ongoing performance enhancement
