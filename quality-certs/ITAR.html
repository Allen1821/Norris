About ITAR

ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations) governs the export and import of defense-related materials, services, and technical data. Being ITAR-compliant means we follow strict U.S. government regulations to protect sensitive defense information and hardware, ensuring national security and customer trust.

Why ITAR Matters

  • Compliance with U.S. export controls
  • Secure handling of defense-related data
  • Eligibility to work on sensitive government contracts

Our ITAR Commitment

Norris Precision Manufacturing is fully registered under ITAR, ensuring that our operations and data handling practices meet the stringent security protocols required by U.S. government regulations. Our dedicated teams and robust security measures ensure compliance and safeguard critical information.
