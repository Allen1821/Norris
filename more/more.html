Pressure Testing

Our state-of-the-art pressure testing facility performs computerized hydrostatic pressure testing up to 7500 PSI using various fluid media. This critical capability ensures component integrity and performance under extreme conditions.

We utilize advanced testing equipment with precise control systems to verify component performance across a wide range of operating conditions, providing comprehensive test documentation for complete traceability.

EDM Capabilities

Norris Precision's in-house EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) capabilities add another dimension to our comprehensive manufacturing processes. Our EDM equipment enables us to machine complex geometries and hard materials that would be difficult or impossible with conventional machining methods.

EDM technology allows us to create intricate features, tight tolerances, and superior surface finishes in even the hardest materials, making it ideal for aerospace and medical applications where precision is paramount.
