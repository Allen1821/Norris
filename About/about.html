About Norris Precision

Over 40 years of excellence in aerospace, military, and medical manufacturing

Our Story

Norris Precision Manufacturing has been a trusted partner in the aerospace, military, and medical industries for over 40 years.

Founded in 1978, Joe Norris Sr. used his experience, expertise, and unwavering dedication to create and develop one of the finest aerospace manufacturing companies in the world. "To stay ahead of your competition you must keep up with technology in every aspect of your business" — a statement which Founder/CEO Joe Norris Sr. and President Joe Norris Jr. consider the backbone of their company's success.

Operating out of a modern 60,000 sq. ft. facility in Clearwater, Florida, Norris Precision employs an array of manufacturing capabilities to ensure customer satisfaction. From the latest 5-axis machining centers and Nadcap accredited metal finishing to the finest ERP and quality management systems, our commitment to quality is unparalleled in the industry.

Our Journey

1978

Norris Precision Manufacturing founded by Joe Norris Sr. in a small 5,000 sq. ft. facility

1985

Expanded operations and moved to a 15,000 sq. ft. facility to accommodate growing customer base

1995

Achieved first ISO 9001 certification, marking our commitment to quality management

2005

Moved to current 60,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art facility in Clearwater, Florida

2010

Obtained AS9100 certification for aerospace manufacturing excellence

2015

Expanded capabilities with addition of 5-axis CNC machining centers

2022

Achieved Nadcap accreditation for special processes

Today

Continuing to innovate and expand our capabilities to serve our customers' evolving needs
