CNC Machinist

Clearwater, FL Full-time

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Aerospace CNC Machinist for our precision manufacturing team. The ideal candidate will have strong technical skills and experience in CNC machining.

Minimum Requirements

  • Must be a United States citizen
  • High School diploma or equivalent education
  • Ability to use basic measurement equipment:
    • Calipers
    • Micrometers
    • Indicators
    • Dial Bores
    • Air Gages
  • Ability to read simplified blueprints
  • Understanding of basic mathematics
  • Basic computer skills
  • Ability to communicate effectively in English
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment
  • Good manual dexterity
  • Ability to lift 30 lbs

Key Skills and Competencies

  • Minimum 5 years' experience preferred for set-up persons
  • 2 years' experience for machine operators
  • Experience in setting up and operating:
    • 3 Axis Vertical CNC Mills
    • 4 & 5-Axis Horizontal Mills
    • Mori Seiki with Fanuc controls
    • Grob G350 with Siemens controls
  • Knowledge of:
    • Tool holders
    • Cutting tools
    • Boring heads
    • Fixtures
    • Inserts
    • Other accessories
  • Ability to utilize gages and equipment for part accuracy inspection
  • Detect and adjust machines to correct setup problems
  • Follow written and verbal instructions with high attention to detail
  • Knowledge of G and M codes
  • Understanding of CNC mill programs
  • Maintain required records
  • Maintain a clean and safe work environment

Company Requirements and Benefits

  • Pre-employment Drug Screening/Background Check
  • Full-time: 40 hours per week, with overtime available
  • Shift Options:
    • 1st Shift
    • 2nd Shift (10% shift differential)
  • Benefits:
    • Health Insurance
    • Dental Insurance
    • Vision Insurance
    • Optional Life Insurance (available after 60 days)
    • 401k (available after 90 days)
    • Paid Holidays
