  • Feasibility: Tighter tolerances available depending on part geometry and material.
TRN

CNC Turning Tolerances

  • Diameter: Typical +/- 0.0005"
  • Length: Typical +/- 0.0001"
  • Surface Finish: 32 RMS or better achievable
SIZE

  • 5-axis Mill: 35" x 27.6" x 27.6"
  • Vert/Horiz Mill: Up to 40" x 22" x 24.8"
  • Turning: 12" diameter x 24" length
  • Wire EDM: 28.7" x 22.8" x 11.8"
