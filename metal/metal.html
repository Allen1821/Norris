Advanced Metal Finishing

Norris Precision Mfg. offers the highest quality Nadcap certified chemical processing. We offer chromic and sulfuric anodize, chem-film, and passivation processed to a wide variety of government, military, and customer specifications.

Having this capability in-house greatly reduces manufacturing lead-time while improving quality by having complete process control to maintain dimensional integrity.

Our state-of-the-art finishing facility ensures that every part meets the exact specifications required, with strict quality control measures at every step of the process.

01

Anodizing

Our chromic and sulfuric anodize services provide superior corrosion protection and surface hardening while maintaining tight dimensional tolerances.

02

Chemical Film

Chemical film treatments (Chem-Film) offer excellent corrosion protection and electrical conductivity for aluminum parts while adding minimal weight.

03

Passivation

Our passivation process removes free iron from the surface of stainless steel parts, enhancing corrosion resistance and restoring the inherent corrosion resistance.

04

Quality Control

Comprehensive testing and inspection ensures all finished parts meet or exceed customer specifications and industry standards.
