About Norris Precision

Our Story

Norris Precision Manufacturing has been a trusted partner in the aerospace, military, and medical industries for over 40 years.

Founded in 1978, Joe Norris Sr. used his experience, expertise and hard work to create and develop one of the finest aerospace manufacturing companies in the world. "To stay ahead of your competition you must keep up with technology in every aspect of your business" a statement which Founder/CEO Joe Norris Sr. and President Joe Norris Jr. feel is the backbone of their company's success.

Operating out of a modern 60,000 sq. ft. facility in Clearwater Florida, Norris Precision uses an array of manufacturing capabilities to ensure customer satisfaction. From the latest 5-axis machining centers and Nadcap accredited metal finishing to the finest ERP and quality management systems, our commitment to quality is unparalleled in the industry.
