Website Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Norris Precision Manufacturing collects and uses information when you visit our website at norrisprecision.com.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies (small data files) to:

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use:

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect information independently.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who help operate our website

Data Security

When you contact us through our website, your message is sent via standard email. We don't store personal information on our website - contact requests are simply forwarded to our email system. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You can browse our website without providing personal information
  • You can disable cookies in your browser
  • You can contact us to request removal of information you've submitted

External Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. This privacy policy only applies to our website, not external sites.

Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
